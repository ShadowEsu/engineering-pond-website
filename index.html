01 · Overview

Mission and problem framing

Stakeholders · ponds · sampling efficiency

The three DVC ponds support turtles, ducks, geese, and other wildlife. Observations note murky water, algae on walls, and occasional stressed fauna; circulation and aeration may be limiting factors. Manual sampling has taken 2–3 hours for a team of five across multiple sites—slow for decision-grade monitoring.

We are building repeatable instrumentation (lab capsule, bench IoT, and surface/underwater platforms) so biology and engineering can share aligned datasets—from bacteria cultures at ~30 °C to field temperature, pH, and turbidity traces.

At a glance

  • Pond health: Clear metrics for stewardship of all three ponds.
  • 30 °C capsule: Controlled cultures for bio team protocols.
  • IoT monitor / boat: pH, temp, turbidity, DO, GPS, RC operation (per design reviews).
  • Color pipeline: Test strips + controlled lighting + grading software on GitHub.
  • Funding path: Student-government presentation; ASDVC Environmental Affairs partnership.

05 · Plan

Roadmap and brainstorm

Engineering notes · 27 Feb 2026 docs

Platforms

3D-printable water surveyor boat; Arduino + Raspberry Pi tiers; robotic fish / ROV-style chassis references.

Sensing

Temperature & pH first; turbidity & dissolved oxygen; general hardness testing; ultrasonic ranging where applicable.

Mechanical

Double-wall concepts; waterproof IP67 enclosures; silicone sealant, gaskets, stainless fasteners; 3D-printed shell.

AI / vision

Evaluate camera + model path to read test strip colors vs. lab control swatches (ties to color-grading repo).

Control

PID thermal loop; syringe micro-dosing sketches; induction heating research (Instructables) for lab tooling only—under advisor review.

Blueprint goal

Start with temperature sensor path, validate in Tinkercad/Wokwi, then layer navigation, power, and waterproofing.

Design risks

Weight vs. buoyancy with multiple sensors; speed vs. drag; weather sealing; solar vs. battery budget; smaller hull → faster iteration.

Navigation

Sensor fusion for effective robot navigation — research alongside Adafruit fundamentals and GPS tutorials for waypoint logging.

07 · Systems & Code

Systems under development

Capsule · logging · robotics

30 °C growth capsule

Insulated chamber with viewing ports, power entry, and indicator—paired with resistive heat path (e.g. Kanthal-class wire) and closed-loop control once the temperature controller stage is integrated.

Waterproof bench tanks and IP-rated project boxes under evaluation for wet testing (see resources).

Control electronics

Microcontroller bring-up in simulation then hardware: thermostat lesson analogs in Tinkercad, Wokwi for firmware checks, then capsule wiring with faculty-reviewed power limits.

Field robotics and lab support

Robotic fish / boat concepts for mobile temperature and water-quality mapping; cart-scale prep for pH, scales, and hot-plate workflows supporting bio cultures.

Environmental logging

24/7 temperature & humidity with live display + ThingSpeak (or successor) channels.

Gantt & funding

Timeline with dependencies; student-government deck; need statements for metrics beyond pH/DO/turbidity.

Color analysis

GitHub color-grading system linked to strip imaging hardware experiments.

Bio × Eng

Bacteria capture and growth supported by controlled capsule and shared data model with IO site.
